# FM Mileage Reimbursement Log

A single-page web tool for tracking and printing mileage reimbursement for the University of Michigan Family Medicine Residency Program.

**[Open the Mileage Log](mileage-log.html)**

---

## How to Use

1. Open the log in your browser.
2. Fill in your name, date, and trip details in the table.
3. Add your signature by uploading an image or drawing directly in the browser.
4. Click **Print / Save PDF** to generate a printable reimbursement form.
5. To start a fresh form, click **New Form** (this clears the entries but keeps your signature).

## Data & Privacy

- All data is saved in your **browser's local storage** — no account or login required.
- If you use the same browser on the same computer each time, your prior entries will still be there when you return.
- **No information is stored outside of your browser.** Nothing is sent to a server.
